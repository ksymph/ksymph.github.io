I'm Kelton (as you may have noticed). I'm curious, careful, and a slow thinker. I love to learn and discover new knowledge; you'll find me diving ever deeper into anything that catches my eye.

Retaining and applying that knowledge comes a little less naturally to me though. So I work hard to keep focused, and to build practical foundations for my interests through creating.

To that end, this site is a culmination of years of undirected learning, and a focal point around which I aim to center my efforts. Earlier versions used a homemade web server running off a phone in my living room; now it's statically generated using a simple lua script. I've seen the edges of the world and am now ready to return home.

Check out what I've been doing over in projects, or see what I have to say over in my blog.

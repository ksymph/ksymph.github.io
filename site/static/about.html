Hello! I'm Kelton (as you might have gathered by now), a developer, perpetual potterer, and a proud slow thinker. Curiosity is my primary pillar; I love to learn, and I'm always looking to find answers to questions I didn't know to ask.

Figuring out what to do with all the skills and snippets of knowledge I've picked up along the way comes a little less naturally though. So I'm on a journey to take a break from my wisdom-wicking wanderings, and start making. It's a different kind of learning, but an adventure all the same.

This site comes out out of that journey; it began as a homemade web server running off a phone in my living room, and has since evolved into a custom static site generator running off GitHub pages.
